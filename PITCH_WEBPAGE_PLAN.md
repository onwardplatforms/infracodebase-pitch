## Sections

## Navigation / UX Approach

**Concept:** A journey-based experience, not a long scroll page. Each section is a "slide" that investors navigate through, but feels modern/app-like rather than a traditional deck.

**Options to consider:**
1. **Full-screen sections** with scroll-snap (each scroll moves to next section)
2. **Horizontal slide navigation** with arrow keys / swipe
3. **Vertical scroll with section indicators** (dots on the side showing progress)
4. **Keyboard-driven** (arrow keys, space to advance) like a presentation

**Decision:** Click/arrow-key navigation, full-screen sections, no scrolling. Optimized for in-person investor presentations. Should flow beautifully with keyboard or click controls.
